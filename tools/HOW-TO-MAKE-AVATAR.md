# 🎭 How to make your talking-avatar video (your face + your voice)

This is the ONLY way to get the "animated talking" look from your reference
video, with YOUR own face. It takes about 5 minutes on a free AI website.

## The 2 files you need to upload

Both are ready in this folder — `tools/for-ai-avatar/`:

| File | What it is | Use it for |
|------|------------|-----------|
| `1_MY_PHOTO.jpeg` | Your professional headshot | the "face" of the avatar |
| `2_MY_VOICE.mp3` | Your 17.6s voice recording | what the avatar "says" |

## Recommended tools (pick ONE — all have a free option)

### ⭐ Option 1 — Hedra  (best for photo + your own audio)
1. Go to **https://www.hedra.com** and sign up (free).
2. Create a new video → **Character / Talking Image**.
3. Upload **`1_MY_PHOTO.jpeg`** as the image.
4. For audio, choose **Upload audio** → upload **`2_MY_VOICE.mp3`**.
5. Generate → wait → **Download** the `.mp4`.

### Option 2 — D-ID  (https://studio.d-id.com)
1. Sign up (free trial credits).
2. **Create Video** → upload **`1_MY_PHOTO.jpeg`**.
3. Under the script box, switch to **Audio** → upload **`2_MY_VOICE.mp3`**.
4. **Generate** → **Download** the `.mp4`.

### Option 3 — HeyGen  (https://www.heygen.com)
1. Sign up (free tier).
2. **Photo Avatar** → upload **`1_MY_PHOTO.jpeg`**.
3. Add audio → **Upload** → **`2_MY_VOICE.mp3`**.
4. Generate → **Download** the `.mp4`.

## Tips for the best result
- If the tool asks for aspect ratio, choose **Landscape / 16:9** (fills the hero best).
- Keep the plain background, or pick a dark/red one to match the site.
- A simple front-facing talking head looks great over the site's red theme.
